Welcome to Battleships!

The objective of the game is to sink all the ships in your enemy's fleet.

Each player starts with the same number of ships.

Fire at your enemy by clicking the tiles on the Computer's board.

Fire again if you hit any of your enemy's ship

Your turn ends if you miss

First player to reduce opponent's score to 0 wins!

Start by clicking on any tiles on the right!
